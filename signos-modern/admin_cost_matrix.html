Override Rules:
  • INHERIT (TRUE): Uses the global Master Data engine rate. If the master rate updates, this product updates instantly.
  • DEACTIVATE (FALSE): Zeros out the cost completely for this specific product, removing it from the margin calculation.
  • CUSTOM: Injects a hard numerical fallback specific to this product's calculations, overriding the master rate.
